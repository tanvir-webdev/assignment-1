Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh excels in frugal innovation, renewable energy, digital transformation, microfinance, and community-driven solutions.

Sustainability

Bangladesh prioritizes climate resilience, renewable energy, green practices, sustainable agriculture, and inclusive economic development.

Infrastructure

Bangladesh is advancing infrastructure with modern bridges, highways, ports, urban development, and improved connectivity.

Empowerment

Bangladesh fosters empowerment through women's education, microfinance, grassroots initiatives, digital inclusion, and community leadership.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to
support economic growth and global competitiveness.
